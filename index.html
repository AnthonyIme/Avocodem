Basics of the Avocode App
Avocode UI is split into three main sections. A panel on the left, a large canvas to preview your designs in the middle, and inspector panel on the right for details about selected layers.

Get code from layer styles
Export CSS, Swift, Android & React Native code from the Styles panel on your right.
